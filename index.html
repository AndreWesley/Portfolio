Hi, I'm André

"The world is my home"

I’m a Brazilian Unity Developer passionate about creating games and interactive experiences.

I was born in Recife, earned my degree in João Pessoa, and completed a postgraduate specialization in Game Development in São Paulo.

I’ve always been curious about how games are built — not just from a creative perspective, but also from the technical side. This led me to dive deep into software architecture for games, seeking ways to turn ideas into polished projects.

I truly admire game designers and how they transform concepts into memorable experiences. My goal is to collaborate with creative teams and contribute with clean, efficient code that brings those ideas to life.

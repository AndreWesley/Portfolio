Dim

This game aims to raise awareness about "Depression Desease".
Created in a 32h game jam, the player control the depression itself and your objective is discourage the victim to do things like play a guitar or watch TV; when the victim see the depression, starts to realize the own condition, then avoid the victim's sight and understand why this situation isn't cool.

Release: 2018
My role: lead programmer
Language: C# (Unity)
